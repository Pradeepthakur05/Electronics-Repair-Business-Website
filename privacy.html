Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026. Learn about how we handle and protect customer contact records and address details.

Welcome to S.K. Electronics & Repairing Centre. Your privacy is critically important to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by our website and how it is used.

1. Personal Information Collection

When you request an appliance repair booking or submit a query through our contact form, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name: To register the service under your name.
  • Phone Number: To coordinate technician dispatch and clarify appliance diagnostic issues.
  • Physical Address: To dispatch our technician to your household or commercial space.
  • Service Details: Information regarding your appliance model and fault description.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is strictly used to process your booking, dispatch technicians, send invoices, and track warranty claims. We do not sell or lease your personal information to third-party marketing companies.

3. Data Security

We implement standard local encryption and security protocols to store and secure contact numbers and address files. Access is restricted to authorized administrative staff and technicians dispatched to your physical location.

4. Contact Privacy Desk

If you wish to have your contact details updated or removed from our local dispatch logs, please reach out to us at:
