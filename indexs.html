Who We Are

About S.K. Electronics

Founded in Kupondol, Lalitpur, we have spent a decade building a reputation for excellence in repairing domestic and commercial electronic appliances.

Providing Professional Repair Solutions Across Lalitpur & Kathmandu

At S.K. Electronics & Repairing Centre, we understand how disruptive a broken refrigerator, a leaking air conditioner, or a faulty oven can be to your routine or business operations. That's why we have structured our operations to deliver prompt, honest, and professional repair services.

We specialize in repairing major cooling systems (AC, deep freezers, double-door refrigerators), domestic utilities (washing machines, water purifiers, geysers), and high-temp food systems (baking ovens, griddles). Over the last ten years, we have diagnosed and successfully fixed over 5000 home and commercial devices.

Our Mission

To minimize client downtime by offering same-day diagnostic visits and transparent, warranty-backed repairs using genuine components.

Our Vision

To become Nepal's benchmark service provider for appliance repair by integrating skilled engineers with high-end diagnostic tools.

The Values We Live By

How we maintain premium quality repair operations across Lalitpur and Kathmandu.

Honest Upfront Pricing

We diagnose the issue first and provide a fixed quote. No hidden fees, no emergency surcharges, and a visitation fee credited towards repair costs.
