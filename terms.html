Last Updated: July 2026. Commercial agreements, diagnostic visitation fees, and service warranty details.

By booking a service or using this website, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms and conditions of S.K. Electronics & Repairing Centre. Please review them carefully.

1. Diagnostic Visitation Fees

A standard diagnostic visitation fee of Rs. 500 applies when our technician visits your premises to inspect the appliance. This fee covers travel cost and expert testing logic.

Fee Credit: If you approve the repair estimate provided by our technician, the Rs. 500 inspection fee is fully waived/credited, and you only pay the quoted repair labor and spare parts costs.

2. Spare Parts & Replacement

All spare parts provided by our technicians are sourced from genuine brand-approved vendors. Invoices for spare parts will be included in the total bill. We do not guarantee repairs executed with client-provided, unverified spare parts.

3. 90-Day Warranty Policy

We stand behind our work with a standard 90-day service warranty. The warranty is applicable ONLY to the specific fault repaired and the exact spare parts replaced. It does not cover any new, unrelated faults that may develop after our visit. Physical damage, water damage, or electrical voltage burnouts after the service date invalidate the warranty.

4. Payment Terms

Payments are due immediately upon completion of the repair work and successful testing of the appliance. We accept Cash, Fonepay/eSewa QR codes, and Bank Transfers.

5. Limit of Liability

While our technicians execute extreme care, S.K. Electronics & Repairing Centre is not liable for indirect damages or pre-existing decay in heavily rusted, old, or faulty electrical lines in client properties. We advise maintaining safety voltage stabilizers for high-load appliances like ACs and refrigerators.
